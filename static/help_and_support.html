Troubleshooting and FAQs

What is Francomobile?

It is a web app developed by a group of students.

Our goal is to let the users learn a new language and have fun at the same time.

How much does Francomobile cost?

It is completely free.

Which languages are out there?

So far we only have French. More languages are coming up.

How do I sign up and log in?

Click the navigation bar and select Get Started. Or you can click here.
